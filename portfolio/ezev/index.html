EZ EV

Lead Visual Designer

EZ-EV, an Exelon program, offers discounts for purchasing and leasing select electric vehicles with participating dealers.

I love cars and getting to work on this project was a blessing. I drew upon all my automotive knowledge to really get these designs just right. I used MotorTrend, Import Tuner and other such magazines to really nail down the artwork.

Please contact me if you'd like to learn more about this project.
